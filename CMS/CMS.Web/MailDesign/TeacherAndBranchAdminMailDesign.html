More about Arpita’s
Arpita's Academy was established in the year 2003 with an objective to guide the students for the board exams of CBSE, ICSE, SSC and HSC. Soon taking into consideration the lack of institutes providing guidance for competitive exams and also on continuous demand, we diversified our portfolio by launching AIEEE & AIPMT in the academic session 2007-08.
Apart from coaching for competitive examination we are also offering program’s for the students of Class VIII to X who appear in Talent Search Examinations like National Talent Search Examination (NTSE), International Olympiads conducted by various Government and Private Organizations.
The Centre began with a small number of students. However to-day it has grown as a premier Institution which guides the students for their success in the challenging Competitive Exams As well as Boards. This is mainly due to the excellent performance of our students and the dedicated efforts of our faculties.
